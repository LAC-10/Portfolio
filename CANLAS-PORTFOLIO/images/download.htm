Open up your gaming universe


Level up your mobile gaming. Gaming Hub is the all-in-one platform to curate the games you already love, discover new favorites, explore what's trending and receive special benefits, all designed to deliver an amazing gaming experience.

All about games.
Anywhere, anytime.


Gaming Hub is the ultimate destination for your mobile gaming adventures. Access and arrange your downloaded games with ease, or discover an extensive collection of new games across various genres on Instant Plays.

Quick, easy game organization


Keep everything organized in Gaming Hub. Manage all your favorite games, game-related bookmarks and images, so you can easily find what you need, when you need it. Plus, your mobile game downloads appear automatically in the app, offering convenient access to your entire collection.

* Some games may not automatically appear in Gaming Hub.

Just click and play.
Simple as that.


Enjoy different games from a wide variety of genres — from classic card games to multi-player shooters — without having to download a single one. Instant Plays offers a diverse assortment of games you can launch with a single click, no installation required.

Check out what's happening


Always stay on top. Gaming Hub is packed with trending games, real-time game rankings and even exclusive offers for Galaxy Store users. No more switching between different apps for trustworthy updates — just get them all here.

* Benefits may differ by game and event content.
